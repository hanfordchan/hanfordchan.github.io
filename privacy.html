Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to improve our app experience:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics such as feature usage patterns and app performance metrics
  • Crash Reports: Diagnostic information when the app crashes to help us fix bugs
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Ensure the app works correctly on your device

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data with analytics services to help us improve the app.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

User Control

You can disable analytics and crash reporting in your device settings or within the app's settings panel.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through GitHub.
