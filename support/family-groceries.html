Family Groceries

Manage your weekly grocery shopping

iOS Android

About

Family Groceries helps you plan and manage your grocery shopping. Create lists, track what you need, and never forget an item again.

Features

  • Create and manage multiple shopping lists
  • Share lists with family members
  • Track items by category
  • Mark items as you shop

FAQ

How do I create a new shopping list?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen to create a new list. Give it a name and start adding items.

Can I share my list with family?

Yes! Open your list and tap the share icon to invite family members to collaborate.

How do I delete an item?

Swipe left on the item and tap "Delete" or tap the item and select "Delete" from the menu.
