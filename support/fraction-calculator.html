About

A powerful calculator that accepts and returns fractions. Perfect for students, teachers, and anyone who needs precise fractional calculations. All results are automatically simplified to their lowest terms.

Features

  • All Operations: Add, subtract, multiply, and divide fractions
  • Order of Operations: Follows PEMDAS (parentheses, exponents, multiplication/division, addition/subtraction)
  • Auto-Simplification: Results automatically reduced using greatest common divisor (GCD)
  • Dual Output: See results as both simplified fractions and decimals
  • Built-in Keypad: Easy input interface for fractions and operators

How to Use

  • Enter fractions using the format: numerator / denominator
  • Use parentheses to group operations: (1/2 + 1/3)
  • The calculator follows standard order of operations (PEMDAS)
  • Results are automatically simplified to lowest terms

FAQ

How do I enter a fraction?

Use the fraction button on the keypad to enter the numerator and denominator separately, or type it in the format "1/2".

Why are my results simplified?

The calculator automatically reduces all fractions to their lowest terms using the greatest common divisor (GCD). This gives you the cleanest, most accurate results.

Can I see the decimal value?

Yes! Every calculation shows both the fraction result and its decimal equivalent.

Does it support order of operations?

Yes, the calculator respects PEMDAS - Parentheses, Exponents, Multiplication/Division, Addition/Subtraction.
